Let Me Introduce To You
Roland Thienpont has a long history in telecom, from senior software designer, over R & D director, to sales and marketing and product manager. In the last ten years he worked in Alcatel-Lucent IP division on the best switching and router products in the industry. Special interest in cloud gaming, cloud security and content distribution networks. In his spare time he enjoys playing guitar and hopes his next album will be as big as Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band.
